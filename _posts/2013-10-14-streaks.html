My name is Steve, and I used to be a binge coder.

I like the feeling of being on top of my life. I like to feel like I am getting always improving myself spiritually, mentally, physically, and socially. In college I joined a society of men who make that their motto. Once I started programming full-time, I found I loved working on side-projects, and I would feel guilty not advancing in my field in my free time.
